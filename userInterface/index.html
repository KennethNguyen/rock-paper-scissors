Rock, Paper, Scissors

In this game, you will be facing an AI.

Rules are as follow:

  • First to five points wins the game!
  • You only get a point if you win. No points allocated in a tie.
  • Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, Paper beats Rock
